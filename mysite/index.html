A new generation of coffee

Coffee is one of the most traded agricultural commodities in the world. Some say it is what makes the world go round. Lets be honest, it is one of the most popular drinks in the world and once you start drinking, it's extremely hard to stop. According to legends, our ancestors, named Oromo, were believed to have been the first to recognize the energizing effects of the coffee plant. Native populations have since then used it as a stimulant for generations to come. Today, we prepare this delicacy in multiple different ways. Let us show you how we make it.
