About Me

“Every child is an artist, the problem is staying an artist when you grow up”
– Pablo Picasso

I have a Bachelors of Science from the University of Illinois at Chicago. I started off my career as an environmental scientist. I enjoyed life, not just on a personal level, but anatomically as well. I've always been fascinated with cellular structures and organisms. This happened during my highschool days in bio class. It wasn't just about the functions, or their necessities to organic life, it was the structural design that really captured my eye. We and everything else around us, are made by design.

After working in the science field for a year and a half, I then realized, that science really was not where I wanted to be. Do not get me wrong, it is most definitely gratifying to repeat a procedure over and over again with little to no mistakes, but being faced with new challenges on a daily basis with the chance to use my creativity? That sounds a hell of a whole lot better.
